# Efficiency Audit (v0.1)

Deliverables:
- uptime + downtime baseline summary
- maintenance history review
- constraints review (power, cooling, controls)
- prioritized recommendations:
  - no-capex (process/maintenance changes)
  - low-capex (parts/controls improvements)
  - retrofit candidate list

Outputs:
- asset_inspection_event reference
- downtime_event classifications where available
